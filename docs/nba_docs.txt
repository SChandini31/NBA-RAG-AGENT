NBA Accreditation Guidelines (2024):

Criterion 1: Vision and Mission
Criterion 2: Program Curriculum
Criterion 3: Course Outcomes and Program Outcomes (CO-PO Mapping)
Criterion 4: Student Performance
Criterion 5: Faculty Contributions
Criterion 6: Facilities and Technical Support
Criterion 7: Continuous Improvement

Format for CO-PO Mapping:
- Matrix with COs in rows and POs in columns
- Map each CO to relevant PO with level (1: Low, 2: Medium, 3: High)

SAR (Self-Assessment Report) Sections:
- Institutional Overview
- Curriculum Details
- Faculty and Staff
- Student Data
- Continuous Improvement Plans
